PROJECT BACKGROUND


MOTIVATION

The original motivation for this subject came from the discovery of a project known as the ‘Android Parking Booking Project’. The main goal of this project was to give users a way to find and reserve parking spaces ahead of time so that they could easily access one when they arrived at their destination location. Seeing the advanced criteria and usefulness of this project we thought it’d be great to not only expand on this idea but popularise it as it could be very helpful with day-to-day tasks, as well as give us a challenge to improve our IT abilities. Being road users ourselves, we understand the frustration that comes with finding a parking space, so this would be a great investment to get rid of what can be a common issue during shopping periods. A few of us drive cars, as such we’d be able to have ideas and suggestions from drivers in the team that will improve the experience of the application, as well as help with making it better for our target audience, being of course those who drive often. By getting a start on this project now, despite the COVID-19 pandemic being a current worldwide issue, we would be getting ahead of any competition. Having an application like this ready for once the world is more free to go outside would be a great opportunity, and give us a ton of users, as many would want to go out and may struggle to find a parking spot once people are able to roam free. We also stress that our goal is not to copy the original project, but rather expand upon the idea and include our own personal features that we feel would benefit.
